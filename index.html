This is a game called Worm. It's very similar to the classic game Snake.

The worm makes compost in the wormery it lives in. Help keep the worm and compost healthy by making sure the worm only eats things that are good for it!

Banana peels and cardboard are great, but plastic isn't.

Click 'Start' to close this info box. When you're ready to begin the game, hit an arrow key to start moving.
